ABOUT

Mohan Kadimpalli is a Chartered Accountant with a law degree and a background in business management, working at the intersection of compliance, governance, and operational strategy. He advises founders, boards, and growing businesses on the regulatory and financial decisions that determine whether a company scales cleanly or accumulates risk.

His practice spans statutory compliance, financial reporting, corporate governance, and regulatory advisory across MSME and agribusiness, retail, beauty and personal care, food and F&B, e-commerce, farmer producer companies, and nonprofit organisations.

Based in Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, with an active practice presence in Mumbai.
